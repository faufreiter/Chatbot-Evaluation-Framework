Chatbot Evaluation Framework

A mixed-methods framework for evaluating expert advisory chatbots.

The framework

What it measures

The Chatbot Evaluation Framework (CEF) combines quantitative survey data and qualitative interview data into a single, transferable framework to evaluate chatbots. It is organised around five components applied in sequence.

Dimension 01
Usability
Dimension 02
Perceived trustworthiness
Dimension 03
Work integration

The five components

  • 00 Demographics form captures participants' informations before the session.
  • 01 Pre-task rating captures expectations before interaction.
  • 02 Tasks are realistic, domain-representative scenarios.
  • 03 Post-task rating records workload and immediate response.
  • 04 Online survey measures usability and trust at scale.
  • 05 Semi-structured interview surfaces reasoning and context.
